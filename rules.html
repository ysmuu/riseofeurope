RiseOfEurope Rules V1

Please read the rules for ingame and Discord, and follow them. Breaking these rules may result in consequences.

INGAME RULES

▶️ A player's behaviour and their previous mutes and bans will impact the length of future punishments.

  • 1. Punishment Evasion
    Do not evade your ban by logging into the server on an alternate account.
  • 2. Appropriate In-Game
    No NSFW Usernames, Skins, or Item Names.
  • 3. For example, block glitching
    Do not abuse exploits or bugs, please report them to staff if you are aware of them! This also means no block glitching!
  • 4. Insiding
    Joining a town just to steal is not allowed. This also counts for killing animals within a claim.
  • 5. Hack clients
    The use of game modifications or 3rd party software to gain an unfair advantage is not allowed.
  • 6. Lag Machine or Bot Raids
    Building machines with the intent to reduce the server's TPS or to crash the server – also known as lag machines – and bot raids are explicitly forbidden.

Please remember to follow the rules to ensure a fun and fair experience for everyone!

CHAT RULES

  • 1. No Discrimination
    ABSOLUTELY NO racism, sexism, homophobia. No exceptions. All cases will result in a permanent mute.
  • 2. No NSFW Content
    No NSFW content is allowed at all. All cases will result in a permanent mute.
  • 3. Follow Discord ToS
    No exceptions. Jokes will be considered as facts. Violations will result in a mute.
  • 4. No Criminal Behavior
    All criminal behavior, including threats and blackmail, is not allowed. Any jokes will be taken seriously. This will result in a ban.
  • 5. No Spam
    Any spam, n-word baiting, empty messages, and over-formatting will be muted. This also includes using channels for the wrong purposes.
  • 6. No Toxicity
    This includes, but is not limited to, ear rape, loud/annoying noises, slander, lies, and insulting language. Offenders will be warned, then muted, then banned.
  • 7. Respect Everyone
    Disrespect of users and staff will result in a mute or ban. Impersonation of staff is also included.

Other Information

Punishment evasion will result in a ban. Exploiting loopholes is not allowed. Appeals can be made to the admin.
